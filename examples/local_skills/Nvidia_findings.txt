Based only on the provided facts, here is what Nvidia is:

Nvidia Corporation is an American technology company founded in 1993 by Jensen Huang, Chris Malachowsky, and Curtis Priem. It is headquartered in Santa Clara, California.

The company develops:
- Graphics processing units (GPUs)
- Systems on chips (SoCs) 
- Application programming interfaces (APIs)

Originally focused on GPUs for video gaming, Nvidia broadened its use into markets including:
- Artificial intelligence (AI)
- Professional visualization
- Supercomputing

Nvidia's product lines include:
- GeForce GPUs for gaming and creative workloads
- Professional GPUs for edge computing, scientific research, and industrial applications

As of early 2025, Nvidia held a 92% share of the discrete desktop and laptop GPU market.

The company became a major player in AI through its CUDA software platform and has controlled more than 80% of the market for GPUs used in training and deploying AI models as of 2025.

Nvidia has expanded into:
- Gaming hardware and services (Shield Portable, Shield Tablet, Shield TV, GeForce Now cloud gaming service)
- Mobile processors (Tegra line for smartphones, tablets, automotive infotainment systems)
- Automotive applications (Drive PX-series for autonomous vehicles)

In 2023, Nvidia became the seventh U.S. company to reach a $1 trillion valuation, and by 2025 had become the first company in the world to surpass $4 trillion and $5 trillion in market capitalization.

Nvidia has been described as a Big Tech company and was selected as one of Bloomberg's "Magnificent Seven" companies based on its strength, size, and market capitalization.
