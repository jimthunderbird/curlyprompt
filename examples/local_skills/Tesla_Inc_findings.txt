Based on the provided facts, Tesla, Inc. is:

An American multinational automotive and clean energy company headquartered in Austin, Texas. The company designs, manufactures, and sells:

- Battery electric vehicles (BEVs) including:
  - Roadster sports car
  - Model S sedan
  - Model X SUV
  - Model 3 sedan
  - Model Y crossover
  - Tesla Semi truck
  - Cybertruck pickup truck

- Stationary battery energy storage devices (home to grid-scale)
- Solar panels and solar shingles
- Related products and services

The company was incorporated in July 2003 by Martin Eberhard and Marc Tarpenning as Tesla Motors. Elon Musk became a major investor and chairman in February 2004, then named chief executive officer in 2008. Tesla began production of its first car model, the Roadster sports car, in 2008.

Tesla is known for being one of the world's most valuable companies in terms of market capitalization and has been the world's most valuable automaker since July 2020. The company is highly vertically integrated, developing many components in-house such as batteries, motors, and software. It also operates a network of Superchargers for vehicle charging and offers services like Tesla Insurance and robotaxi services.

The company has faced various lawsuits, controversies, and criticism related to labor practices, safety defects, regulatory compliance, and public statements by CEO Elon Musk.
