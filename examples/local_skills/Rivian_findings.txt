Rivian is an American electric vehicle manufacturer and automotive technology company founded in 2009. It produces electric vehicles including:
- An electric sport utility vehicle (SUV) called the R1S
- A pickup truck called the R1T
- An electric delivery van called the Rivian EDV (Electric Delivery Van)

Rivian started deliveries of its R1T pickup truck in late 2021. The company is based in Irvine, California, with its manufacturing plant in Normal, Illinois, and other facilities in various locations including Palo Alto, California; Carson, California; Plymouth, Michigan; Burnaby, British Columbia; Wittmann, Arizona; Woking, England; and Belgrade, Serbia. Rivian has plans to build another US$5 billion factory in Social Circle, Georgia.

The company raised over US$13.5 billion in financing following its initial public offering in November 2021, trading under the ticker "RIVN" on the Nasdaq stock exchange.
